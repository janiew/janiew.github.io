Who Am I?

Hi! I am a programmer, game designer, and writer based in Ithaca, NY. I currently am a senior at Cornell University, where I am majoring in Computer Science as well as Feminist, Gender, and Sexuality Studies. Out of class I design tabletop roleplaying games and write everything from poetry to games crit.
